KBTU students tournament

Types of tournament that will be

  • Counter-Strike 2
  • Dota 2
  • Clash Royale

Tournament formats

Counter-Strike 2: 5v5, single elimination, BO1 matches.

Dota 2: 5v5, double elimination, BO1, final — BO3.

Clash Royale: 1v1, Swiss system, best of 3.

Info

Organizers

This tournament is hosted by the KBTU Student IT Club with support from the Faculty of Computer Technology. The main goal is to bring together students who are passionate about esports, help them connect with each other, and organize friendly competitions between teams.

Schedule

  • Counter-Strike 2 Start: 11:00 AM End: 2:00 PM Break: 12:30–1:00 PM
  • Dota 2 Start: 2:30 PM End: 5:30 PM Break: 4:00–4:30 PM
  • Clash Royale Start: 6:00 PM End: 8:00 PM
