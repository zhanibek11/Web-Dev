Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400 g
Ground beef 300 g
Tomato sauce 200 ml
Onion 1 medium
  1. Boil the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Cook ground beef with chopped onion until browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve sauce over spaghetti.

Grilled Chicken Salad

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breast 2 pieces
Lettuce 1 head
Tomato 1 large
Cucumber 1 medium
  1. Grill the chicken until fully cooked and slice it.
  2. Chop lettuce, tomato, and cucumber.
  3. Mix vegetables in a bowl and top with sliced chicken.
  4. Add your favorite dressing.
